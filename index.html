Hello, I'm Sophie Mauran

Postdoctoral Researcher in data assimilation and model reduction applied to oceanography.

My research focuses on ensemble data assimilation, emulation, and model order reduction for oceanography, leveraging machine learning and data-driven approaches. Currently working at Inria Rennes in Odyssey research group.

Research Interests

My research work leverages Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Spaces (RKHS) to address non-linear problems by linearizing complex relationships through implicit feature maps. By evaluating similarity via kernel functions, this modular framework enables the modeling of complex phenomena without explicitly defining the underlying non-linear processes. Highly adaptable, these methods are applied to oceanographic and meteorological forecasting to improve reliability while minimizing computational costs.
